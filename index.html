Data Privacy Notice

CSC Cup 2026 Registration

The Central Student Council collects your personal information and submitted documents for the purpose of processing, reviewing, and verifying your registration for the CSC Cup 2026.

The information collected may include your name, age, course, student ID number, contact information, team selection, medical certificate, notarized parental consent, and other required registration documents.

Your information will only be accessed by authorized CSC Cup organizers and assigned personnel. It will be used only for registration verification, eligibility checking, participant coordination, safety purposes, and other activities directly related to the CSC Cup 2026.

Your personal information and documents will not be shared with unauthorized individuals or used for unrelated purposes. Reasonable measures will be taken to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

By submitting this form, you confirm that the information you provided is accurate and that you consent to the collection and processing of your personal information and documents for the purposes stated above.
